Border

The border goes aroound the padding and content box. They are around every box.

Some have rounded corners or may even look like a circle thanks to changing styles.

We can style all borders, just one, just two or just three. It can be top and bottom, left and right, or any other combination we want.

Border Properties

The properties are border-width, border-color and border-style. The first controls the thickness, the second the color and the third the type of line style(solid, dashed,etc.).

Border radius

This helps us make different shapes. It is normally used with percentages.

a border-radius of 10px will give us slightly rounded corners, but one of 50% will give us a circle.

It can also be applied to only one, two, three or all corners.
