Margin

The margin is the space between a box and the edge of the screen, or between two boxes.

The body has 8px of margin by default, it is common to have it be set to 0px when creating a new website, it can be added later.

Here we have 2 paragraphs with a width of 300px. The first box has a margin of 50px on all sides, the second has no margin other than the default. Now there is space between all sides of box 1.

Box 2 has just a small default margin as we can see when comparing with the background-color.
